Fold Wars

Fold Wars is the first game I developed. It's a minimal tower defense game. The gameplay is inspired by Defense grid. You protect the gold in your mine from enemies that arrive in waves. You build various types of towers to kill them and save your gold. The maps have large open areas to allow you to create mazes of towers to make it difficult for the enemy to reach your gold mine.
