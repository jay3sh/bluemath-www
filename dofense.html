Dofense

Dofense is a Tower Defense RTS with simple goal, but deep strategy. Protect six energy cores from the pink monsters. Destroy their gate before they overwhelm you. With every battle, gain crystals and use them to craft your strategy. Make and share your own battles in easy-to-use level editor.
